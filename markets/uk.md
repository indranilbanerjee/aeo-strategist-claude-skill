## UK-Specific AEO Notes

- **Companies House number** is a uniquely powerful UK trust signal. Embedding company registration number in content and schema provides a verifiable government-linked entity anchor.
- **FCA Register** — for any financial services firm, FCA registration number and the FCA register link is essential. AI checks this for BFSI credibility queries.
- Post-Brexit: EU regulatory approvals (GDPR, CE marking) are no longer automatic — UK-specific equivalents (UK GDPR, UKCA marking) must be referenced separately.
- NHS trust / CCG mentions for healthcare — being referenced in NHS documentation is UK's equivalent of government health authority citation.
