## Key AEO Notes

- **ABN (Australian Business Number)** visible in content and schema is a strong government-linked entity anchor — equivalent to India's CIN number.
- **TGA certification** for healthcare/pharma is the Australian equivalent of CDSCO — essential trust signal.
- Australian consumers are highly review-sensitive — ProductReview.com.au carries significant weight for B2C brands.
- New Zealand: NZBN (NZ Business Number), FMA (financial regulation), Medsafe (pharma) are the equivalent anchors.
