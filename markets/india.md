## AI Platform Landscape
- **Google Gemini**: Dominant (Google holds >95% search share in India). AI Overviews rolling out. Most critical platform for all Indian clients.
- **ChatGPT**: Fast-growing in metro urban / English-proficient segment. Significant for B2B and startup ecosystem.
- **Perplexity**: Growing among startup, tech, and research audiences. Under-served — early mover advantage.
- **Meta AI (WhatsApp)**: Extremely high penetration via WhatsApp. Dark social AI — unobservable by any tracking tool. Growing fast in tier-2/3 cities. No direct optimization possible yet.
- **Google Assistant / Siri**: Voice queries — often regional language. High volume but low commercial intent tracking.
- **Jio AI / Airtel AI**: Telco-level AI integrations emerging. Watch for 2025–2026 expansion.

## India AEO-Specific Insights

### The Vernacular Opportunity
India has 22 officially recognised languages and ~500+ million internet users who are not primarily English-speaking. Current AEO work is almost exclusively English-optimised. This creates a first-mover opportunity:
- **Hindi** (600M speakers): "best home loan hindi", "mutual fund me invest kaise karen"
- **Bengali** (100M speakers, incl. Bangladesh): Howrah/Kolkata real estate, healthcare, education queries almost entirely unoptimized
- **Tamil** (80M speakers): Chennai real estate, manufacturing, healthcare
- **Marathi** (83M speakers): Mumbai/Pune real estate, FMCG
- **Telugu** (82M speakers): Hyderabad real estate, pharma, IT
- **Gujarati** (55M speakers): BFSI, manufacturing, MSME queries
- **Kannada** (45M speakers): Bangalore tech, healthcare, real estate

**Recommendation for INT TechShu clients**: Any client with significant regional audience should run parallel AEO tracks in English + primary regional language. The competition is essentially zero.

### Wikidata Gap
Indian brands are severely under-represented in Wikidata. For any Indian brand that has been operating 5+ years, creating or completing a Wikidata entry (with proper sameAs links to Indian directories + LinkedIn + CIN number) is among the highest-ROI AEO actions available. Takes 2–4 hours, provides permanent parametric equity.

### Tier-2 / Tier-3 City Opportunity
Most AEO content targets 8 metros. Cities like Lucknow, Jaipur, Indore, Surat, Coimbatore, Patna, Bhopal, Vishakhapatnam have AI query volumes that are completely unoptimized. For real estate, healthcare, and local services clients with tier-2/3 presence, this is a white-space opportunity.

### Government Portal Visibility
Being listed on government portals is an extremely high-trust AEO signal unique to India:
- MCA portal (company registration) — visible and trustworthy
- Udyam/MSME portal
- RERA state portals
- SEBI / IRDAI registrant lists
- FSSAI licensee database
- BIS licensee database
Ensure clients appear on all relevant government portals with correct, current information.

## Platform-Specific India Notes

### Gemini / Google AI Overviews
- Rolling out in India; English first, Hindi expanding
- Google Business Profile is disproportionately important — complete it for all local clients
- YouTube content is cited in ~25% of AI Overviews — India's YouTube user base (500M+) makes this especially valuable

### ChatGPT
- Bing index coverage in India is weaker than Google — prioritise IndexNow for faster Bing crawl
- Particularly strong for English-language B2B queries from Indian startups

### Meta AI / WhatsApp
- No direct optimization path currently — but brand name accuracy across the web helps
- Meta AI uses Bing as its search engine — same Bing optimization applies
