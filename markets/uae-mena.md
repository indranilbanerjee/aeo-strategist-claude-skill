## UAE / MENA AEO-Specific Notes

### Arabic Language Opportunity
Despite Arabic being the official language of all MENA countries, most brand AEO work is English-only. Arabic queries in ChatGPT and Gemini are growing rapidly but poorly optimized. For any client with MENA consumer audience, Arabic content strategy is a high-priority AEO opportunity. Particularly:
- MSA (Modern Standard Arabic) for formal/business content
- Gulf Arabic dialect signals for consumer content
- Consider right-to-left (RTL) schema and technical implementation

### Dubai as Global Hub
Dubai functions as a proxy for international credibility. A brand that is featured in Gulf News, listed with Dubai Chamber, and DFSA/RERA registered appears credible not just in UAE but across MENA and for South Asian diaspora globally. This amplification effect is unique to Dubai's media ecosystem.

### Ramadan / Religious Seasonality
Ramadan drives massive query volume shifts in MENA. Content and query tracking must account for peak Ramadan periods (March-April, date shifts annually). Halal certification becomes especially visible.

### Expat Segment
Dubai has ~85% expat population. Content must often serve both local Arabic-speaking audience and English-speaking expat/professional segment simultaneously. Different trust signals matter for each.
