## SEA AEO Notes

### Language Diversity = First-Mover Opportunity
SEA has some of the world's largest internet populations (Indonesia: 200M+ users, Philippines: 80M+) but almost no AEO content in local languages. Bahasa Indonesia and Thai AEO are near-zero competition spaces.

### Singapore as SEA Trust Proxy
Similar to Dubai in MENA, Singapore acts as a regional credibility hub. A brand covered in CNA, listed with MAS, and featured in Tech in Asia appears credible across the entire SEA region. For regional HQ brands, Singapore signals amplify into all SEA markets.

### Mobile-First Reality
Indonesia, Philippines, Vietnam: mobile-first markets. Voice search and conversational AI queries dominate over desktop search. Content must be structured for conversational answer extraction, not just page-browsing.

### Super-App AI (Emerging)
Grab, Gojek, Sea Group (Shopee/SeaMoney) are building AI features into super-apps. These are dark social AI equivalents for SEA. Monitor for 2025–2026 expansion. No direct optimisation path yet, but brand name accuracy across the web (consistent entity signals) helps.
