## Europe AEO Critical Note: GDPR

**GDPR is not just a compliance issue — it's an AEO trust signal.** For EU-facing content:
- Privacy policy must be current, specific, and linked prominently
- Cookie consent management is increasingly checked by AI systems
- Data residency statements ("data stored in EU-based servers") are trust signals
- GDPR DPA (Data Processing Agreement) availability for B2B SaaS is essential
- Post-EU AI Act 2024: AI products must reference compliance with the Act for EU enterprise clients

## Europe AEO Notes

### Local Language Priority
Germany, France, Spain: local language AEO content is substantially higher value than English. German-language AI queries are growing fastest among all non-English European languages. For German clients, German-language structured content is priority.

### Sustainability Signal Weight
European AI users have the highest sensitivity to sustainability claims globally. ESG reports, environmental certifications (ISO 14001, B Corp, EU Ecolabel), and carbon neutrality commitments carry meaningful AEO weight for EU-facing brands in all verticals.

### EU AI Act Implications (2024)
First-of-its-kind regulation. High-risk AI applications (healthcare, recruitment, critical infrastructure) have documentation requirements. For AI product companies targeting EU, compliance documentation is itself an AEO trust signal — publish it visibly.
