## INDIA

### AI Platform Landscape
- Google Gemini is dominant (Google has >95% search share)
- ChatGPT growing fast, especially metro urban and English-proficient segments
- Perplexity growing among startup/tech/research audience
- Voice queries (Google Assistant, Siri) — regional language queries are often voice-first

### Tier-1 Publications with High AI Citation Weight (India)
**Business/Finance (AI-licensed or high-DA)**:
- Economic Times, Business Standard, Mint (Hindustan Times Group)
- LiveMint, Financial Express, Business Today
- Forbes India, Inc42 (startup/tech)
- Moneycontrol, NDTV Profit

**Sector-specific**:
- Pharma: Express Pharma, Pharmabiz, ET Healthworld
- BFSI: Outlook Money, BankBazaar Blog, PolicyBazaar Blog
- Real Estate: Times Property, Magicbricks News, 99Acres Blog
- Tech/SaaS: YourStory, Techcircle, MediaNama, The Ken

**Regional/Language Publications** (emerging AEO opportunity):
- Hindi: Navbharat Times, Dainik Jagran (digital)
- Bengali: Anandabazar Patrika (online), Ei Samay
- Tamil: The Hindu Tamil, Dinamalar
- Marathi: Maharashtra Times, Lokmat

### India-Specific Directories by Vertical
**General Business**: IndiaMART, JustDial, IndiaBizFor, Sulekha
**BFSI**: BankBazaar, PolicyBazaar, Paisabazaar, Coverfox
**Real Estate**: MagicBricks, 99Acres, Housing.com, NoBroker, Makaan
**Healthcare/Pharma**: Practo, 1mg, PharmEasy, Lybrate, Netmeds
**SaaS/Tech**: Nasscom marketplace, G2 India section, Capterra India
**Retail/E-commerce**: Flipkart, Amazon India, Meesho (seller presence)

### Regulatory Bodies as Trust Anchors
- SEBI (Securities and Exchange Board of India) — BFSI
- RBI (Reserve Bank of India) — Banking
- IRDAI (Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority) — Insurance
- CDSCO (Central Drugs Standard Control Organisation) — Pharma
- RERA (Real Estate Regulatory Authority) — state-specific, project-level
- BIS (Bureau of Indian Standards) — manufacturing/retail
- FSSAI (Food Safety and Standards Authority) — F&B/health food
- NASSCOM — tech sector credibility
- TRAI — telecom

### India AEO-Specific Notes
- **Vernacular gap is massive**: Most AEO work targets English queries. Hindi, Bengali, Tamil, Marathi queries are poorly optimized — this is a first-mover opportunity for any client with regional audience
- **Local entity signals**: District/city-level government mentions (municipal approvals, local authority registrations) carry weight for hyperlocal real estate and retail
- **WhatsApp/closed AI**: India has extremely high WhatsApp usage; AI features in WhatsApp and Meta AI are essentially dark social AI — unobservable but growing rapidly
- **Wikidata**: Indian brands are severely under-represented in Wikidata — creating/completing entries here is one of the highest-ROI AEO actions for any Indian brand

## Multi-Market Client Approach

When a client operates in 2+ markets:
1. Build separate query libraries per market (queries differ even for same intent)
2. Separate directory targets per market
3. Single canonical English description + localized variants per language
4. Platform prioritization differs: build Gemini-first for all markets, then add market-specific priority
5. Compliance: always apply the strictest constraint from any market the brand operates in
6. One RERA/FCA/SEBI registration doesn't cover another market — verify each jurisdiction separately
